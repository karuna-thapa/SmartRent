Our Story

Who We Are

SmartRent was founded in 2021 with a simple vision: make renting a vehicle in Nepal as easy, transparent, and stress-free as possible. We saw that travellers and daily commuters alike were struggling with outdated booking processes, hidden fees, and limited choice — so we built something better.

Today we operate across Nepal's major cities and tourist routes, offering a curated fleet of cars, bikes, and scooters maintained to the highest safety standards. Whether you need a rugged 4×4 for a mountain road trip or a nimble scooter for city errands, SmartRent has you covered.

Our platform connects verified local vendors with customers, creating a trusted marketplace that benefits both sides — fair prices for renters, reliable income for vehicle owners.
